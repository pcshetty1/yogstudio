5 Benefits of Daily Yoga

1. Increased Flexibility: Daily stretching improves posture and joint health.

2. Reduced Stress: Breathing and meditation calm the nervous system.

3. Improved Sleep: Evening yoga routines relax the body and mind.

4. Enhanced Focus: Mindful movement sharpens mental clarity and awareness.

5. Boosted Immunity: Consistent practice balances hormones and reduces inflammation.

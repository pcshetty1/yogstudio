Breathing Techniques (Pranayama)

Breath is life. In yoga, pranayama techniques teach us to regulate breath for mental clarity and emotional stability.

Try Nadi Shodhana (alternate nostril breathing) for balance, or Ujjayi (victorious breath) for focus. Box breathing is excellent for managing anxiety.

Just 5 minutes a day of focused breathing can shift your mood and physiology profoundly.

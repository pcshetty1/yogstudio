Morning Yoga Routine for Energy

Mornings set the tone for the rest of the day. A simple 10–15 minute yoga sequence can energize your body and center your mind.

Start with deep breaths in a seated position. Transition to cat-cow stretches, gentle twists, and standing poses like Mountain, Warrior, and Forward Fold.

Conclude with a short savasana or seated meditation, affirming your intentions for the day ahead.

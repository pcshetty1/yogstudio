Meditation is not about escaping the mind, but learning to sit with it in stillness. Mindful meditation allows us to witness thoughts without judgment and return gently to the present moment.

Begin with 5 minutes of silence each morning. Focus on your breath. When your mind wanders (and it will), simply return to the inhale and exhale.

Guided meditations, body scans, or even mindful walking are excellent ways to build this practice. The key is consistency over intensity.
